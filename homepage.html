about second opinion

The primary reason for a second opinion is to reduce the risk of medical errors by allowing another specialist to evaluate the radiology scan. Each year, tens of thousands of patients die due to medical mistakes.
It may help detect minor or major errors from the initial diagnosis, and allowing doctors to determine the best possible treatment for each patient.
